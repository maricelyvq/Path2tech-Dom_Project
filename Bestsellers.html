1

Lost In Space

by Cynthia Macmiller

Ava Carter has always been the perfect daughter—the brilliant young Black woman who landed a coveted role as a NASA data scientist straight out of grad school. But beneath the accolades and stability, she feels trapped. The numbers and codes don’t move her the way music does. Late at night, she finds herself lost in the hypnotic beats of melodic techno and the soul-stirring rhythms of R&B, secretly crafting mixes and dreaming of a life behind the decks. Will she stay in orbit, bound by expectations, or risk it all to chase the music calling her soul?

Genre: Contemporary Fiction, Coming-of-Age    Published: January 09, 2024

2

The Red Rose

by Pricolis McKell

Daniel's love life is a disaster. He chases fleeting desires, not real needs. Then Sasha, stunning and witty, arrives at his office, igniting a fire he can't control. She's everything he thinks he wants, except for one crucial detail: she's married to his boss. Will Daniel learn to distinguish between fleeting pleasure and lasting happiness, or will the seductive bloom of the red rose conceal thorns that pierce deeper than he ever imagined?

Genre: Drama, Contemporary romance    Published: April 24, 2023

3

Bytes and Barriers

by Lynn Harthford

After years of stability as a high school math teacher, Lynn Harthford takes a leap of faith into the fast-paced world of software engineering. But the transition isn’t easy—financial struggles, an overwhelming learning curve, and moments of self-doubt threaten to derail her dream. Part guidebook, part personal story, Bytes and Barriers is a raw and inspiring look at what it really takes to reinvent your career, proving that success isn’t just about talent. It’s about perseverance, support, and the courage to start over.

Genre: Nonfiction, Self-Help    Published: April 25, 2024

4

The Tiger In The Shadows

by Matthew Holloway

Deep in the forests of Southeast Asia, King, a rare Malayan tiger, lives a life of constant danger. Hunted by poachers and feared by humans, he is forced to stay in the shadows. But when he stumbles upon a terrified young girl, Amira, in the hands of traffickers, instinct takes over he protects her, forging an unbreakable bond. She names him King, a symbol of the strength she sees in him.A gripping tale of survival, resilience, and an unlikely friendship, The Tiger In The Shadows explores the beauty of the wild and the power of those who fight to protect it.

Genre: Literary Fiction, Survival    Published: September 04, 2023
